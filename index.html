About Koderz Club

The Department of Computer Science & Information Technology NED has always aimed to polish the technical skills and to nurture expertise for its undergraduate students by conducting credible coding competitions for all the CS & IT Programmes.

Thus, Koders Club, an official Computer Science & Information Technology community was born in 2022, with a sensational mission of enticing all the tech-enthusiasts to boost their competence and problem solving skills.


Our Objective

To organize coding competitions, workshops, seminars, hackathons and other events that foster a culture of innovation and excellence.

Our Vision

To become a leading community of coders and innovators in the region and to contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

15th May - 9th June

Participation Criteria

  • At maximum 4 students in a group.
  • Participation of all the third year students is mandatory. The sessional marks (Theory - 10 , Practical - 10) of web engineering course will be rewarded to the students based on their performance in this competition.
  • First year, second year, and Final year students can also participate in this competition.
  • Each group needs to register for one of the four themes listed below.

    • 15th May 2023

    Wait is Over!

    Students can choose from Four Exciting Tracks each supervised by a Faculty Member

  • Conference Website Design (Lead: Ms.Samia)
  • Student Coding Club Website Design (Lead: Ms.Saadia)
  • Scholarship Portal Design for the Students of NEDUET (Lead: Dr. Murk Marvi)
  • Students-Industry Linkage Portal (Lead: Dr. M Mubashir Khan)


    • 22th May 2023

    The competition officially begins

    Students can now start working on their solutions. For assistance, a Whatsapp Group has been made where all the leads will be available to answer any queries. All registered participants should join using the link below.

    Link: Whatsapp Group

    9th June 2023

    Submission Day.

    Submission Guidelines:

    • Teams should email a Zip File containing all of their working to their lead.
    • Zip File should be named according to the following format:
      • TeamName-LeadName
      • For example: TeamPluto-MsSaadia
    • Emails of leads are provided on their respective Track Details page.
    • For Evaluation Day, Students should prepare a Demo Video showcasing all the requirements of their selected track.

    Link: Track Details

    15th June 08:30 AM - 2nd May 4:30 PM

    Evaluation Day.

    • Reporting time for all teams is 8:30 AM Sharp.
    • Evaluation will start at 9:00 AM sharp.
    • Teams will be called in random order with each team having 15 mins to present their project.
    • Each team will be called out once and no repeat chance will be provided incase of being late.
    • Final Result will be announced at 12:30 PM
    • Prize Distribution will be carried out by OctDaily at 1:00 PM in Main Auditorium

    Location: Petroleum AV Hall
