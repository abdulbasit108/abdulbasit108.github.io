Conference Website Ms. Samia

Your task is to create an innovative and practical website for an IEEE research conference. You can draw inspiration from various international and national conference websites to develop your own unique and optimal design. The website must contain the following content:

  • A dynamic Home Page
  • About us page with host organization and conference introduction.
  • Call for Papers page with (CFP Poster and Important Dates), Submission Process and guidelines for authors.
  • Registration page for registration related information and forms. Conference Program (containing schedule)
  • Conference Committees Page (Technical Committee, Organizing Committee etc)
  • Gallery (for pictures of all previous events)
  • Archives that will allow access to all previous conference webpages (all previous websites should have all these pages plus a new Proceedings page containing information of all published papers and conference proceedings book)
  • Sponsors & Contact Us page
Lead Contact: samia@neduet.pk

    Students-Industry Linkage Portal Dr. Mubashir

    This portal will have the following features that will help the department admin to connect students with the industry and vice-versa. This portal will provide

    • A dashboard to the Chairman and Office Staff to view the current students' details and their related information.
    • An interface for gathering/collecting CVs/Resumes, LinkedIn profile URL, WhatsApp numbers, and email addresses of students.
    • A facility to instantly call on the WhatsApp number of any student.
    • A facility to share any job-related document with students on multiple channels (emails, WhatsApp, LinkedIn etc ... )
    • A facility to send selected details if students with the relevant industry
    • An Interface where students can write about their skillset and the nature of their job of interest.
    • New job advertisements from the industry.
    • and more interesting features as it will evolve.
    Lead Contact: mubashir@neduet.pk
